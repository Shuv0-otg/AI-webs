HOME WORKOUT

Warm-Up · Every Session · 5 mins
2 min — Jumping jacks
1 min — Arm circles
1 min — Hip rotations
2×10 — Light push-ups
1×15 — Bodyweight squats
Push-ups 4 × 12–20
Body straight, chest to floor. Pause 1 sec at bottom for max tension.
Decline Push-ups 3 × 10–15
Feet elevated on chair. Targets upper chest and front delts.
Pike Push-ups 3 × 8–12
Hips high in a V. Lower head toward floor. Shoulder-dominant movement.
Chair Dips 3 × 10–15
Slow controlled tricep dips. 3 sec down, 1 sec up. Elbows stay back.
Backpack Rows 4 × 12–15
Squeeze shoulder blades at top. Hold 1 sec. Slow descent.
Towel / Bedsheet Rows 3 × 10–12
Knot a bedsheet over door, grip and row your body in. Lower = harder.
Reverse Snow Angels 3 × 15
Face down. Sweep arms overhead in wide arc. Targets rear delts + mid-back.
Bodyweight Bicep Curls 3 × 12–15
Use backpack or isometric curl. Slow 3-sec negative. Full squeeze at top.
Bodyweight Squats 4 × 15–20
Full depth. Chest up. Pause at bottom. Drive through heels explosively.
Lunges 3 × 12 each
Big step forward, rear knee hovers above floor. Keep front knee over ankle.
Wall Sit 2 × 60 sec
90° at knees. Back flat on wall. Breathe slowly. Embrace the burn.
Full Body Fat-Loss HIIT
4–5 rounds · max intensity · no equipment
High Knees → Mountain Climbers → Burpees → Rest 45 sec
Repeat 4–5 times. No mercy. This is your fat incinerator.

PROGRESSION

01
Add reps weekly
Push for 1–2 more reps every week before increasing sets.
02
Add sets over time
Once reps plateau, add an extra set to spike total volume.
03
Slow the tempo
3 sec down, 1 sec up. Slower = more tension = more growth.
04
No progress = no result
Doing the same thing as last week means your body has no reason to change.
